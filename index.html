  StudyMate Agent Chat
Capabilities
Not a chatbot. A study agent.
🧠

Multi-Step Planning

The agent breaks your goal into sub-tasks, reasons through each step, and executes them sequentially — just like a study tutor would.

🔍

Live Web Research

Uses Gemini's grounding with Google Search to find the latest study materials, YouTube lectures, and practice problems for any topic.

📅

Calendar Scheduling

Automatically creates Google Calendar events for each study session via MCP, sending you reminders and time-blocking your week.

📊

Progress Tracking

Maintains a local database of topics studied, quiz scores, and weak areas — then adjusts the plan dynamically as you progress.

✉️

Email Summaries

After each session, the agent emails you a summary of what you covered, what's next, and resources to review.

🎯

Exam Simulation

Generates custom quiz questions based on your syllabus, evaluates answers, and identifies knowledge gaps to target.
